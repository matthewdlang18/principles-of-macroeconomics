How to Play

Investment Odyssey is a simulation game that teaches the fundamentals of investment and portfolio management. Here's how to get started:

  1. Choose your mode - Single player for practice or Class game to compete with classmates
  2. Manage your portfolio - Buy and sell assets to maximize your returns
  3. Respond to market changes - Watch for price movements and economic events
