How to Play

For Students:

  1. Enter your name and the class number provided by your TA.
  2. Join the game and start managing your portfolio.
  3. Buy and sell assets to maximize your returns.
  4. Check the leaderboard to see how you compare to your classmates.

For TAs:

  1. Access the TA Dashboard.
  2. Create a new class with a unique class number.
  3. Share the class number with your students.
  4. Start the game and manage rounds from your dashboard.
  5. View class-specific leaderboards to track student performance.
