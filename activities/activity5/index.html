What to Expect

        
        
        

            
Teaching Practice Mode:

            

                

                    
1. AI Student Response

                    
The AI will attempt to answer your question but make realistic mistakes, like confusing concepts or getting the direction of effects wrong.

                

                

                    
2. Your Teaching Moment

                    
Correct the AI's mistakes with clear explanations. Use examples, explain cause-and-effect relationships, and connect to other economic concepts.

                

                

                    
3. AI Feedback

                    
Receive a grade (1-10) and specific feedback on what you explained well and what could be improved.

                

            

        


        
        

            
Multiple Choice Creator Mode:

            

                

                    
1. Conceptual Inspiration

                    
The AI uses your original question to identify core concepts, then creates a new question exploring a different angle or application of those concepts.

                

                

                    
2. Your Answer & Explanation

                    
Choose your answer and explain your reasoning. This helps reinforce your understanding and identifies any knowledge gaps.

                

                

                    
3. Verified Answer & Explanation

                    
The AI provides the correct answer with a detailed explanation that you can copy and submit to the course Google Form.
